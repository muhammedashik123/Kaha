The atelier

A small house that believes a garment should be worn a hundred times, not once.

Founded in the lanes of Chalappuram, Kaha Designs works the way couture once did — fabric chosen by hand, embroidery drawn before it is stitched, fittings taken in person. The palette leans into the things Kerala wears well: deep indigos, mocha browns, festive golds. Nothing loud, everything considered.

The hand

What we still do by hand

i

Aari & thread embroidery

Drawn onto the fabric, then worked needle-by-needle — the detailing that gathers compliments without ever shouting.

ii

Gold bootis & scalloped edges

Delicate metallic motifs and finished borders, the signatures that mark a Kaha piece across a room.

iii

Custom fit & matching codes

Bridal attire, party wear and family dress-codes, measured and stitched to the body rather than to a size chart.

iv

Considered fabric

Pure cottons, georgette and seasonal festive silks — chosen wholesale and retail for how they fall and last.
